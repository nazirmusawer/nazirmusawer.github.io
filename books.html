Books

Below is a list of books that have made a deep and lasting impact on me. As the adage goes, “your thoughts become your destiny” and these books have influenced my thoughts.

The First and Last Freedom

Jiddu Krishnamurti

About 1/4 of the way into this book I was really wondering if I could make it through what seemed like ramblings; a guru with more style than substance. But later I began to see how deeply he dissects the mind.

The Road to Serfdom

F. A. Hayek

This is a towering work. Though I only recently read it, it aligned with thoughts I had years ago about power, control, and how “good intentions” can still build cages.
